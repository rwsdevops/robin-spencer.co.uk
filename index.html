About

I am a councillor and technology leader with a background in transformation, platforms, delivery, and governance.

I care about practical change, thoughtful leadership, and making services easier to understand and use. My work sits at the point where strategy, operations, and people meet.

I am neurodivergent and dyslexic. I think quickly, connect ideas fast, and work best with clear structure, honest feedback, and a strong sense of purpose.

I believe good leadership means combining strategic thinking with empathy, clarity, and a focus on real outcomes.

Working style
